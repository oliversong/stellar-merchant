Integrating with Astral

Include this script on your web page:

<script src="https://www.astralproject.us/static/js/astral.js" type="text/javascript"></script>'
<script>
  Astral = new Astral('{{g.user.authToken if g.user else "AUTH_TOKEN"}}');
</script>

Call this after someone has bought a gift card:

Astral.buAstralGiftCard('CARD_ID')
Where CARD_ID is your gift card's ID.

Call this when someone is paying with a gift card:

Astral.payWithAstral('PRICE')
Where PRICE is their purchase amount in USD. For example, 20.
